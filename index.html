Hi! I'm Anqi Yang.

I'm a 2nd-year PhD student in the ECE department at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Aswin C Sankaranarayanan.

I completed my Master in Robotics Degree in Robotics Institute at CMU, co-advised by Prof. Aswin C Sankaranarayanan and Prof. Srinivasa Narasimham. Before coming to CMU, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from School of Software Engineering, Tongji University, where I worked with Prof. Lin Zhang. I was a receipient of Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship.

My research interest lies in the intersection of computational photography and deep learning. My recent research focus on building high-quality under-panel cameras through innovating display layouts and optimizing new optical elements. The avatar on the left was captured and reconstructed from an under-panel camera I developped.
